Favourite Animals Kept as Pets

Dogs

Dogs are generally regarded as mans best friend. Most are kept as guard dogs to protect their owners. Some are kept as house pets because they are small, fluffy and cute. They require regular vaccination.

Cats

Cats are the next in line. Although, they are not protective creatures, they can be great companions. They eat little and are more easy to clean.

Fishes

Fishes are little pets. Eat less but need frequent cleaning unless they have a filter.
